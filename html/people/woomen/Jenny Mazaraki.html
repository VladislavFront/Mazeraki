The power of the serve, the power of the mind: the Sara Mazarakis story

Sara Mazarakis is a dedicated student-athlete from Sydney, Australia, currently representing Midwestern State University in NCAA Division II tennis. As a sophomore pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering, Sara combines her academic achievements with her performance in tennis as a key member of the MSU Mustangs women's team.

Before enrolling at MSU, Sara attended Meriden School in Sydney, where she developed a passion for tennis and achieved early success in junior tournaments. She later moved to the United States to continue her education and athletic career. At the university, Sara has excelled both on and off the court. In the 2025 season, she formed a highly successful doubles pair with Sydney Williams, securing a 9-2 record, a 3rd place finish, and winning six consecutive matches. In September 2023, she reached the "B" final at the ITA South Central Regional Championship, defeating several higher-rated players along the way.

Sara's academic accomplishments mirror her athletic success. She has earned a place on the Dean's Honor Roll and the Lone Star Conference Commissioner's Honor Roll, reflecting her commitment to excellence in all aspects of her university life.
