Men Woomen

Jenni Mazaraki is an Australian writer and researcher whose work navigates the intricate terrain of motherhood, identity, and the everyday. Based in Melbourne, on Wurundjeri land, she brings a multidisciplinary perspective to her writing, drawing from her background in visual arts, communication, and psychoanalytic thought. She is currently completing a PhD in Creative Writing at La Trobe University, where she explores the representation of motherhood in contemporary literary fiction. Her academic background includes:
- Master of Art Therapy – La Trobe University
- Bachelor of Visual Communication – Monash University
- Bachelor of Fine Art – RMIT University
