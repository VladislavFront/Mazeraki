Modern design with a Greek soul: the story of Anna Maria Mazaraki

Anna Maria Mazaraki is a famous Greek jewelry designer and the founder of the eponymous brand. Her name has become synonymous with the concept of "affordable luxury" designed for a wide audience. At the core of her work is the belief that jewelry should reflect a woman's individuality and emotions.

Her journey into the world of jewelry began in 1995 when she started creating unique pieces for her friends. What began as a creative passion soon evolved into a full-fledged business, driven by the desire to create special adornments.

Striving to combine rich Greek heritage with modern design, Anna Maria launched the "Anna Maria Mazaraki" brand and opened her first store in the Athenian suburb of Chalandri, laying the foundation for a future success story.
