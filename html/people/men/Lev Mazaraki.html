Lev Mazaraki: the magic of the frame calling for discovery

Lev Mazaraki is a travel photographer whose work inspires journeys and deep understanding of the world. His passion for visual art and exploration became a vocation that connected him with many cultures. He continues to explore the planet, skillfully capturing its atmosphere and essence through the camera lens.

From a young age, Lev was drawn to photography and the art of visual storytelling. This passion led him to study at an educational institution where he honed his artistic vision, studying composition and lighting. He showed particular interest in analog photography, experimenting with film and expressive compositions. One of his early projects helped him deeply understand various cultures, which played a key role in shaping his professional path.

His passion for photography took Lev on distant journeys, where he sought to capture the diversity of cultures, landscapes, and human experiences.
