Men Woomen

Lev Mazaraki is an acclaimed travel photographer known for his ability to capture the soul of diverse cultures and landscapes. With a refined sense of composition and a passion for storytelling through images, he has established a career that bridges adventure, visual arts, and education. From an early age, Lev was immersed in an environment rich in history and creativity. This background inspired his interest in photography as a tool to document and express the world around him. He pursued formal education in visual arts, where mentors nurtured his curiosity and encouraged experimentation, shaping his distinctive artistic voice.
