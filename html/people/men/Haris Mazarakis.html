Men Woomen

Haris Mazarakis is a Greek director, screenwriter, and composer renowned for his work in television and film. Over the span of more than two decades, he has developed a multifaceted career, contributing to some of the most critically acclaimed Greek series and films through compelling storytelling and atmospheric direction. He began his artistic journey in 1996 with the short film *Prin to telos* (*Before the End*), which he wrote and directed. This early work set the tone for a career defined by psychological depth and narrative experimentation.
