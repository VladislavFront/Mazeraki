Haris Mazarakis: the multifaceted talent of Greek cinema and television

Haris Mazarakis is an outstanding Greek director, screenwriter, and composer known for his significant work in both television and film. Over more than two decades of creative activity, he has built a multifaceted career, contributing to the creation of many popular Greek series and movies. His works are characterized by captivating storytelling and atmospheric direction, allowing him to deeply immerse viewers in the worlds of his stories.

Haris Mazarakis began his creative path in 1996 with the short film “Prin to telos” (translated as “Before the end”). He not only directed this work but also wrote the screenplay. Even in this early project, the hallmarks of his style emerged: psychological depth and bold narrative experimentation, which set the tone for his entire subsequent career.

Among his well-known recent works are the series “Sta syrmata” (On the Wires, 2023) and “Aoratoi” (The Invisible, 2022), where he served as both director and screenwriter. In some projects, Haris Mazarakis also acts as a composer, underscoring his versatility and deep understanding of all aspects of cinematic production.
